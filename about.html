    Summer means hitting the beaches and enjoying the heat of summer. The town of San Juan, La union has fast become the Surfing Capital of the Nothern Luzon. San Juan La union is one of the premier surfing destinations in our country. District has waves for all levels of surfers. After riding the waves, you can sit back, relax and watch one of the best sunsets in Luzon.
