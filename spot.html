    Gefseis Greek Grill

    As its name suggests, Gefseis Greek Grill offers an authentic taste of Greek cuisine. It is one of the well-loved La Union restaurants because of its Greek-inspired setting and of course, its fresh and tasty dishes. More importantly, this restaurant uses absolutely no preservatives in all their dishes. How healthy is that, huh?

    Tagpuan

    Satisfy your Pinoy combos craving at Tagpuan. This Filipino eatery is popular for its comforting dishes at very reasonable prices. You can buy the most affordable food choices out of all La Union restaurants in this establishment. Also, have a good late-night drinking session with your friends here!

    El Union Coffee

    Probably the most famous coffee shop located in San Juan, El Union Coffee serves the finest beverages in town. Its laid back ambiance encourages every visitor to make a good conversation over a cup of good coffee and scrumptious desserts. So, add this local coffee shop in your where to eat in La Union list.

    Makai Bowls

    4Makai Bowls where to eat in la union Image Credit: @ilovenem via Instagram Looking for a place to get a healthy snack? Visit Makai Bowls, one of the healthier choices of La Union restaurants, which is conveniently at Flotsam and Jetsam Hostel! Ask anyone where to eat in La Union and they’ll point you here. You’ll surely be delighted with its delish smoothie bowls. Their bowls are 100% sugar-free, dairy-free, and made with real fruits!

    Mad Monkeys

    Have a taste of the flavorful and mouthwatering burgers of Mad Monkeys when in San Juan. This burger joint is sure to serve you a curated selection of their dishes. Also, this restaurant invites some local artists to perform all day and night, which is really something to look forward to.

    Flotsam and Jetsam

    Known to be a hostel, Flotsam and Jetsam kitchen is one of the more popular La Union restaurants too that celebrates music, art, and delectable international cuisine. One of its popular dishes is its specialty pizza, which is definitely a must try. Plus, the whole airy, hipster establishment is Instagram-worthy. No one could resist taking photos here, seriously. It’s a great place to check out when searching where to stay in La Union as well!

    Olas Banditos

    Owned by one of the hottest surfers in the country, Luke Landrigan, and his friends, Olas Banditos is a newly opened Mexican restaurant in the heart of San Juan. Serving you your favorite nachos, burritos, quesadillas, tacos, and other Mexican food, this is one of the best places where to eat in La Union. Go and check it out!

    Surf Shack

    The go-to spot of where to eat in La Union for all foodies, surfers, and beach bums is no less than Surf Shack. It is a relaxing hub where you can grab appetizing food and drinks. You can also catch their live music every weekend for a more fun experience.

    Angel and Marie’s Place

    This cozy little place in San Juan will surely have you a surfer’s vibe. Angel and Marie’s Place is one of the first standalone La Union restaurants in town. It offers different types of dishes, including pasta, seafood, breakfast food, halo-halo, and fruit shake.

    Halo-Halo de Iloko

    Where to eat in La Union can’t just be central to San Juan.That’s why you should check out this restaurant in San Fernando and indulge in their authentic Ilokano cuisine. Of course though, what’s there to try in Halo-Halo de Iloko other than their famous Fiesta Halo-Halo?! Rest assured that everything tastes really good!

    Coast Call Kitchen and Bar

    Inside the San Juan Surf Resort is a perfect spot to unwind and taste yummy food. Coast Call Kitchen and Bar is one of those La Union restaurants that has everything from pizzas to rice meals. It is simply a nice place to enjoy the rest of the day while munching on great food.
